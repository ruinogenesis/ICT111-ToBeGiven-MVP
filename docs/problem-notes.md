
# Problem Notes

Early notes on the problem areas our team is exploring. These are rough observations to prepare for Lab 2 opportunity scanning.

## Problem Area 1: Tracking crypto airdrop tasks across many protocols
- **Who has the problem:** Retail crypto users and airdrop hunters who farm many protocols at once.
- **Why it matters:** Missing a single task or snapshot date can mean losing a reward worth real money.
- **Existing alternatives:** Manual spreadsheets, Twitter/X bookmarks, Discord notifications, memory.
- **Gap:** No single dashboard shows all campaigns, tasks, and deadlines in one place.

## Problem Area 2: Tracking degree progress and prerequisites
- **Who has the problem:** University students managing their own course plans in credit-based programs.
- **Why it matters:** A missed prerequisite can delay graduation by a full semester.
- **Existing alternatives:** PDF curriculum sheets, advisor meetings, personal spreadsheets.
- **Gap:** No simple tool calculates remaining credits and flags unmet prerequisites automatically.

## Problem Area 3: Tracking content across social platforms
- **Who has the problem:** Small content creators and micro-influencers posting on TikTok, Instagram, and YouTube.
- **Why it matters:** Lost track of posts means missed repurposing opportunities and weaker consistency.
- **Existing alternatives:** Notes apps, memory, scattered spreadsheets.
- **Gap:** No single board shows each content idea's status and platform at a glance.

## Common Thread
All three problems are about scattered, fast-changing information that has no single clear view. This supports our team direction of building a simple web-based tracking dashboard.
